Shipping Policy

This Shipping Policy explains how ThinknShop (“we”, “our”, “us”) processes and ships customer orders placed on our platform.

1. Order Processing

  • All products are manufactured or prepared after the order is confirmed.
  • Standard processing time is 7–10 business days.
  • Orders are not processed on Sundays or public holidays.
  • During peak seasons, processing timelines may extend.

2. Shipping & Delivery

  • Orders are shipped using trusted logistics partners.
  • Estimated delivery time varies depending on location.
  • Tracking information is shared via SMS or email after dispatch.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges, if applicable, are displayed at checkout.
  • Charges depend on product weight, size, and delivery location.

4. Address Accuracy

  • Customers must ensure correct shipping details while placing orders.
  • ThinknShop is not responsible for failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses.
  • Re-shipping charges may apply if the order is returned due to address issues.

5. Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, delays may occur due to unforeseen logistics issues, weather conditions, or regional restrictions.

6. International Shipping

  • International delivery timelines and charges vary by destination.
  • Import duties, taxes, and customs charges are the responsibility of the customer.

7. Support

For shipping-related queries, please contact our support team with your Order ID.

Email: support@thinknshop.in
