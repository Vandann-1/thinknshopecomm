Shipping Policy

All ThinknShop products are made to order. Once you place an order, the item is produced specifically for you to ensure quality and accuracy.

Order Processing Time

  • Orders take 7–10 business days to process.
  • No processing occurs on weekends or public holidays.
  • During high order volume, processing time may increase.
  • You will be notified via email or phone if there is a significant delay.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Orders are shipped through reliable courier partners after processing.
  • Delivery time varies based on your location.
  • Tracking information is shared once the order is dispatched.

Delays & Address Issues

  • We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect addresses.
  • Please double‑check your details before placing the order.
  • If a package is returned due to an incorrect address, reshipping charges may apply.

International Shipping (If Applicable)

  • Shipping charges and delivery timelines vary based on destination.
  • Custom duties or import taxes are the customer’s responsibility.
